Frequently Asked Questions in Recruitment

Here are the most common questions and answers about our recruitment and manpower services.

What are the requirements for hiring domestic workers?

Requirements usually include a valid ID, a signed recruitment contract, and any necessary governmental approvals depending on your country’s laws.

How long does the recruitment process take?

The process typically takes 1 to 3 months depending on the country of origin, required documents, and processing time from authorities.

Do you provide a warranty period for the recruited worker?

Yes, most recruitment contracts include a warranty period during which the worker can be replaced in case of valid reasons.

Which countries do you recruit from?

We recruit from several countries including the Philippines, India, Bangladesh, Kenya, and more based on client requirements.

Can I track the recruitment process?

Yes, we provide regular updates and allow you to track the recruitment progress from start to arrival.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, online payments, and in-office cash payments according to your convenience.
